# ⚔ THORNBOUND
### A brutal dark fantasy chronicle where five kingdoms hang by a thread, and every choice you make pulls at it.

---

## What is this?

Thornbound is an AI-powered dark fantasy text RPG. Five kingdoms. One fragile peace. You choose who you become — and the realm remembers everything.

The AI narrates a living world that reacts to your decisions. Allies die. Plans fail. Moral choices echo forward. There are no clean hands here.

---

## Features

- **4 Origins** — Bastard Knight, Court Scholar, Sellsword, Fallen Noble. Each shapes your stats, inventory, and starting position.
- **4 Eras** — Gathering Storm → Great Betrayal → War of Five Thorns → The Reckoning. The world escalates whether you're ready or not.
- **6 Endings** — King, Shadow Hand, Exile, Martyr, Iron Warlord, Great Deceiver. Earned, not chosen.
- **Character portrait** — your silhouette visually corrupts as Darkness rises. Cracks, shadow bleeding, eye glow.
- **NPC relationship tracker** — trust/fear/hostility for all 6 cast members, updated by the AI every turn.
- **Parchment scroll chronicle** — your choices logged as an aged manuscript, grouped by era.
- **Realm map** — live political map of the Five Kingdoms, your location highlighted each turn.
- **Ambient music** — Kevin MacLeod tracks shift per era and mood. Danger sounds different from dread.
- **Death screen cinematic** — eulogy-style ending with your pivotal choices, final stats, and an AI-written epitaph.
- **Save/load** — your run persists between sessions via localStorage. Pick up where you left.

---

## How to run

Just open `thornbound.html` in any browser. No install. No build step. No server.

To play it online, host the file anywhere — GitHub Pages, Netlify, any static host.

---

## Setup

You need a free Gemini API key from [aistudio.google.com](https://aistudio.google.com).

1. Open `thornbound.html` in Notepad
2. Find `PASTE_YOUR_GEMINI_KEY_HERE`
3. Replace it with your `AIza...` key
4. Save and open in browser
